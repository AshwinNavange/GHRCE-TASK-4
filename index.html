Our Facilities

GHRCE provides exceptional facilities that cater to the academic, personal, and professional growth of its students, including well-equipped laboratories, advanced computer systems, modern classrooms, and a well-stocked library.
The campus also boasts of excellent sports facilities, including a cricket ground, football ground, basketball court, and gymnasium, which provide students with a range of opportunities to engage in physical activities and sports. The campus is also equipped with state-of-the-art medical facilities, ensuring the safety and wellbeing of its students.

World Class Library

The GHRCE library is a modern and well-stocked facility that provides students with a vast collection of books, journals, and other reading material, along with access to online resources, making it an excellent resource for academic and personal development.

Large Play Area

The GHRCE playground is a well-maintained and spacious facility that offers students ample opportunities to engage in a range of outdoor sports and activities, including cricket, football, basketball, and more, providing a healthy and enjoyable environment for physical activity and recreation.

Air Cooled Cafeteria

The GHRCE cafeteria is a spacious and modern facility that offers a wide range of healthy and delicious food options to cater to the diverse dietary needs of its students, making it a popular spot for students to unwind and socialize.
