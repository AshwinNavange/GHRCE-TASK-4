About Us

Established in 1996, G H Raisoni College of Engineering [GHRCE] is a premier Autonomous institution in the central India imparting
a holistic technical education to the students residing not only in India but also international students. The institution has
always been ranked amongst a well performing institution by National Institutional Ranking Framework (NIRF), MHRD, Government of India.
